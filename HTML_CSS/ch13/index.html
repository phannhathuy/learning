Whether you’re looking for the latest smart technology or a powerful instrument to explore your creative side, Yoga’s got you covered. Available in sophisticated Slate Grey and Light Silver, the Yoga Slim 7 Pro is crafted from premium aluminum. Thoughtful details include a raised camera notch for easier opening with one hand, and a backlit keyboard with new dome-design keypads that enable a more comfortable typing experience. Make the absolute most of your visuals, with 2.8K clarity on a 14" display featuring 90% screen and a taller ratio aspect for optimized viewing when scrolling web pages or working on documents.
